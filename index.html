Welcome to WallGo!

How to Play:
- Two players, each with two pieces.
- Place your pieces on the board, alternating turns.
- Move one of your pieces up to two spaces per turn (up, down, left, or right, no diagonals).
- After moving, click "Place Wall" to place a wall next to your piece.
- Walls block movement. Try to enclose your pieces in the largest area!
- The player with the largest combined area at the end wins.
